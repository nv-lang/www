Sponsors

Corporate sponsorship of the Nova project opens after the 0.1 release.

Not yet active. Corporate sponsorship opens after the Nova 0.1 release. This page is a placeholder; sponsor logos and the sponsorship offering will appear here once the release ships.

What we’ll offer

After 0.1, corporate sponsors will get:

  • Logo placement on the homepage and in the README of the main repository.
  • Sponsor mention in release notes.
  • Direct line to the maintainer for design feedback and roadmap input (advisory, not binding — project sovereignty is preserved per our published governance).
  • Optional design-partner pilot — a paid engagement where we build something specific on or around Nova for your team.

Why wait for 0.1

A serious sponsorship discussion needs a serious product. We don’t want a sponsor to write a cheque against a roadmap; we want them to evaluate a release. Our published sponsorship roadmap is explicit about this: no active outreach to companies before 0.1.

Preliminary contact

If you’re potentially interested and want to be notified when sponsorship opens — email hello@nv-lang.org. We’ll keep a list and reach out on release day.

Individual financial support is open today via /support/.
